Hello, I'm Liu

Bakelite (Phenolic Resin) Components · 15 Years Manufacturing Experience

I run Hualing Machinery, located in Hengshui City, Hebei Province, China, specializing in Bakelite (phenolic resin) mechanical components for 15 years. From apprentice to independently managing the entire production line — material formulation, mold design, mass production, quality inspection — I've encountered and solved more problems than most.

This blog is where I organize and share 15 years of hands-on experience: which phenolic resin grades are right for which working conditions, material performance differences between grades, the most common mold design pitfalls, and the quality details you can't afford to ignore during inspection.

Our factory produces approximately 2 million Bakelite components annually, exporting to over 50 countries and regions. If you have custom requirements or technical questions, feel free to reach out anytime.
